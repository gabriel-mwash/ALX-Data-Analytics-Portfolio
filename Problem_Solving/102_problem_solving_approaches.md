## Approach to solve the problem
For our problem, we would employ the design thinking and pragmatic thinking approach

## Here's why
Real world problems are not easy problems. They are ill structured, and complex. In fact real problems, like the one we have, are hard to solve because:
1. We have to agree that its a problem
2. We need to understand the problem
3. There is no one solution to the problem
4. The problem constitutes of unknown but related variables
5. and lastly its almost inpractible to determine wether these problems have been solved

Real world problems are real, and therefore affect humans at a personal level, and what better way than design thinking to approach a problem
as its human centered and fosters collaboration, not only with stakeholders, but with other related variables to address the problem.

## Design Thinking
Design thinking is a human centered and collaborative approach towards solving ill structured and complex problems. The user is always on the engine of the solution, 
to ensure that the problem is solved and no gaps left behind.
